FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is Bombay Investors Club?
Bombay Investors Club is a private members' community centred around investing, exceptional conversations, and lifelong learning. We bring together thoughtful investors to explore businesses, markets, emerging trends, and investment opportunities through discussion and shared learning.
Who is membership intended for?
Membership is designed for individuals who enjoy thoughtful discussion, intellectual curiosity, and long-term thinking. Our community may include investors, operators, founders, professionals, and others who contribute meaningfully to the quality of conversation.
What kinds of discussions happen at the Club?
Conversations may span businesses and investment case studies, public markets, emerging ideas, technology, macro trends, geopolitics, and selectively curated opportunities. Topics evolve based on member interests and guest speakers.
Are investment opportunities shared?
Members may periodically gain exposure to selectively curated opportunities. These are intended to encourage learning and thoughtful discussion as much as investing, and are explored collectively through debate and evaluation. Bombay Investors Club is not an investment platform or syndicate.
How selective is membership?
Membership is intentionally curated. Our objective is to maintain a high quality of dialogue, diversity of perspectives, and an environment where members can learn from one another.
What is the membership fee?
Annual membership is currently ₹30,000. Specific events or experiences have separate participation fees.
How can I join?
Applications are currently open for founding members. Request membership through the application form and we'll be in touch regarding next steps.
